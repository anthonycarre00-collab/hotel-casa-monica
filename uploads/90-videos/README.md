# 🎥 Videos Folder

## Important: Read this before uploading videos

Videos need **careful analysis** before being added to the site. Unlike images, videos:
- Significantly increase page load time
- May autoplay (which can be jarring)
- Need a poster image (the still frame shown before play)
- May have audio that needs muting or subtitling

## What the assistant will do with videos

When you upload videos here, the assistant will:
1. **Analyze each video** — extract key frames, describe content, identify if it's scenery/event/people
2. **Consider carefully** whether to use it — only high-value videos that add real atmosphere will be used
3. **Extract a poster frame** — a still image to show before the video plays
4. **Compress and optimize** — videos are re-encoded to web-friendly format (H.264 MP4, ~720p, ~2-5MB)
5. **Add to the site** in one of these ways:
   - As a **background video** (muted, autoplay, loop) for a hero or section header
   - As a **click-to-play video** in the gallery with a poster image
   - As a **link to Instagram reels** (if the video is already on Instagram, we link out instead of hosting)

## Good candidates for video

✅ **Use these:**
- Slow panning shots of the river at sunset
- Drone flyovers of Mompox
- Short clips of musicians playing (jazz festival, cumbia)
- Processions during Semana Santa
- Cooking moments (casabito being made, etc.)
- Quiet moments on the patio (rocking chair, fan spinning, coffee being poured)

❌ **Avoid these:**
- Anything longer than 30 seconds (keep clips short — 5-15 seconds is ideal for backgrounds)
- Shaky handheld footage
- Videos with loud background noise that can't be muted
- Videos with people who haven't consented to be on the website
- Vertical phone videos that are mostly black bars when cropped to landscape

## File formats

- **Preferred**: `.mp4` (H.264 video, AAC audio) — works everywhere
- **Acceptable**: `.mov`, `.webm`, `.3gp` (will be converted)
- **Avoid**: `.avi`, `.mkv` (poor browser support)

## What's already available

The hotel's Instagram (@casamonicamompox) has reels — these can be linked to directly without hosting the video files. If a reel is particularly good, mention it and the assistant will consider embedding it as a link instead of a hosted video.
