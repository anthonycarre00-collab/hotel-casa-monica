# 01 — Owners & Family Portraits

Solo portraits and family group photos of Fredy, Mónica, and family members.

## Especially needed

- **Solo portrait of Sra Mónica** — currently using an illustrated placeholder
- **Updated solo portrait of Sr Fredy** (the current one is him by a statue — would be nice to have one at the hotel)
- **Family group photo** — Fredy, Mónica, and any children/family who help run the casa

## Ideal specs

- **Orientation**: Vertical (portrait mode) — 4:5 ratio is perfect
- **Resolution**: At least 800×1000 pixels
- **Lighting**: Natural, warm (golden hour or soft window light)
- **Background**: At the hotel — doorway, patio, rocking chairs, or colonial wall
- **Expression**: Genuine smile, relaxed, not stiffly posed

## Filename hint (optional)

If you name files clearly, the analyzer will pick up the hint:
- `monica-solo.jpg`
- `fredy-portrait.jpg`
- `family-group.jpg`

But filenames aren't required — the AI vision will figure out who's who.
