# 06 — Events & Festivals

Photos from local events in Mompox — these give the site a sense of place and seasonality.

## Events to look out for

- **Festijazz Mompox** (September/October) — jazz festival with concerts in plazas and churches
- **Semana Santa** (Holy Week, March/April) — processions, Serenata a los Difuntos, Paso Robado
- **Christmas** (December) — lights, nativity scenes, festivities
- **Festival Totó la Momposina** (July) — cumbia, bullerengue, traditional music
- **Cumbión** — town-wide parade
- **Filigree workshops** — artisans at work
- **River processions** — boats on the Magdalena

## What works well

- Live performance shots (musicians mid-song, instruments in hand)
- Crowd scenes with atmosphere (candlelight, colored lights, fireworks)
- Processions with the colonial architecture as backdrop
- Detail shots (a musician's hands, a candle, a flower arrangement)

## What to avoid

- Blurry low-light phone photos (common at evening events — try to find sharper ones)
- Photos with identifiable children who aren't family
- Anything that feels intrusive to a religious ceremony
